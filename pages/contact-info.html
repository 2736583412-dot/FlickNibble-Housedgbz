Our Story

Founded in 2018, Sakura Elite Experiences was born from a passion for sharing the authentic beauty of Japan with the world. Our founders, having spent decades immersed in Japanese culture, recognized the need for premium, culturally-sensitive tourism that goes beyond typical sightseeing.

We specialize in creating bespoke journeys that blend traditional Japanese culture with modern entertainment and sports experiences. From private tea ceremonies with master artisans to VIP access at sumo tournaments and exclusive anime studio tours, we craft unforgettable memories.
